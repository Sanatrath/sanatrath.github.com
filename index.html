Who is Sanat Rath?

Sanat is an Interaction Designer, Illustrator and Usability Researcher from India with more than 5 years of freelancing experience with start-ups and industry leaders.

He is currently pursuing his Master's in Human Computer Interaction at the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta. His hobbies are designing and playing badminton.

Skills

  • Wireframing / Prototyping
  • Visual Design / Illustration
  • Evaluation / Cognitive Walkthrough
